# Quicksand Eyes

We thought it was fantasy.
We were such fans, you see,
watching her dance so free.
Her personality
lit up the dark brightly.
We talked and became friendly.
After a month or three,
our feelings grew massively.
It’s beyond our control, you see.
We’ve sunk into her quicksand eyes.
